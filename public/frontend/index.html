// KNOWLEDGE BASE

IPL FACTS

🚀
BORN IN 2008
IPL was launched on April 18, 2008 by BCCI under commissioner Lalit Modi. The first match was played between Kolkata Knight Riders and Royal Challengers Bangalore in Bangalore.
💰
BILLION-DOLLAR BEAST
The IPL brand is valued at $16.4 billion (2023), making it one of the most valuable sports leagues globally — ahead of the NHL and MLB in terms of per-match value.
🌍
GLOBAL VIEWERSHIP
IPL 2023 averaged 9.5 million viewers per match on JioCinema alone. The tournament is broadcast in over 120 countries, reaching 600+ million fans worldwide each season.
🏆
MOST TITLES
Mumbai Indians hold the record with 5 IPL championships (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020). Chennai Super Kings follow closely with 5 titles as well (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023).
🏏
RUN MACHINE
Virat Kohli is IPL's all-time leading run scorer with 8000+ runs. He hit a record 973 runs in a single season (2016), including 4 centuries — a feat still unmatched.
💎
AUCTION RECORD
Sam Curran became the most expensive player in IPL auction history when Punjab Kings bought him for ₹18.50 crore in the 2023 mega auction, breaking all previous records.
⚡
SHORTEST FORMAT
Each IPL match is a T20 contest — 20 overs per side. Matches are completed in ~3.5 hours, making it cricket's most action-packed format with big hits and dramatic finishes.
🎯
WICKET WIZARD
Yuzvendra Chahal leads the IPL wicket charts with 200+ wickets. Lasith Malinga held the previous record and famously took 4 wickets in 4 consecutive balls.
📺
MEDIA RIGHTS MEGA-DEAL
In 2022, IPL media rights were sold for a record ₹48,390 crore (approx $6 billion) for 5 years to JioCinema (digital) and Star Sports (TV) — the second most expensive sports rights globally.

IPL HISTORY

2008
The Birth — IPL Season 1
Rajasthan Royals defeated CSK in a thrilling final. Shane Warne captained the underdogs to glory. Brendon McCullum smashed 158* off 73 balls in the first ever IPL match.
2010
CSK's First Dynasty Begins
Chennai Super Kings under MS Dhoni won their first title, starting a dynasty. Sachin Tendulkar won the Orange Cap (618 runs). Mumbai Indians debut at the top.
2013
Mumbai Indians — Champions for the First Time
Rohit Sharma's MI defeated CSK in a nail-biting final. Dwayne Bravo's last-over heroics failed by 23 runs. Rohit started his journey as cricket's most successful IPL captain.
2016
Kohli's Historic Season
Virat Kohli scored an unprecedented 973 runs in one season — 4 centuries, 7 fifties. Still the single greatest batting performance in IPL history. SRH won the title.
2018
The Comeback Kings Return
After a two-year ban (2016–17), CSK returned and won the title. Ambati Rayudu and Faf du Plessis powered them to glory. Called one of cricket's greatest comeback stories.
2019–20
MI's Back-to-Back Dominance
Mumbai Indians became the first team to win back-to-back IPL titles. The 2020 season was played entirely in UAE due to COVID-19 — the first time IPL moved fully overseas.
2022
Gujarat's Dream Debut
New franchise Gujarat Titans (led by Hardik Pandya) won the IPL in their very first season — only the second debut-season winners after Rajasthan Royals (2008).
2023
Mega Auction & CSK's 5th Title
Sam Curran sold for ₹18.5 crore — the most expensive auction bid in IPL history. CSK won their 5th title in MS Dhoni's farewell season, scripting a fairy-tale ending.
2024
KKR Dominate; RCB Finally Win
Kolkata Knight Riders won IPL 2024 emphatically with Shreyas Iyer as captain. RCB finally clinched their first ever WPL title. The league broke viewership records again.
